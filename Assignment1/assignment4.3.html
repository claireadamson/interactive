The lucid and seigniorial aigrette of vertigo with invisible brow, scintillates, then shadows
a stature dainty tenebrous erect, in its siren torsion, time to slap
with impatient scales ultimate bifurcated, a rock, false manor
right away evaporated in mists that imposed a limit on infinity.
